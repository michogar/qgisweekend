QGISWeekend

November 29 & 30, Santiago de Compostela

A weekend to enjoy QGIS

Paolo Cavallini

Paolo Cavallini is Founder of Faunalia a company specialized in FOSS4G between Italy and Portugal. He is Member of QGIS Project Steering Commitee with the role of Financial/Marketing Advisor. Co-founder and past president of GFOSS.it - Italian Geographic Free and Open-Source Software Association has a big implication in many of comunity movements around the free software for geomatic. Charter member of OSGeo - Open Source Geospatial Foundation * international foundation and Co-founder of ITPUG - ITalian PostgreSQL User Group. Over 10 years working around the free and open source GIS software. Paolo will be here to share all his knowledges about the QGIS project with us.

Victor Olaya

Victor is the creator and main developer of the QGIS Processing Framework, and evolution of the SEXTANTE geospatial analysis library that is now integrated into QGIS as a core plugin. He has contributed to the PyQGIS cookbook and is a frequent lecturer on QGIS and spatial analysis. He works at Boundless, where he is currently developing QGIS plugins that allow to interact from QGIS with OpenGeo Suite and the distributed versioning system for spatial data GeogGit.

Friday 29th November, 18h to 21h

Know QGIS Project with Paolo Cavallini

Do you know QGIS Project?, do you know the benefits using it in your projects?. Paolo Cavallini will share with us all he knows about the QGIS Project. After a brief presentation will have a time to discuss with Paolo doubts, interests and whatever you want about the QGIS project.

Free registration is required

Saturday 30th November, 10h to 20h.

QGIS development with Victor Olaya

If you ever wanted to learn how is developing in QGIS, Victor Olaya will help you to start with this. During all this day he will teach you how prepare the development enviroment and how make your first features. We will learn the details of the QGIS software architecture.

Free registration is required
